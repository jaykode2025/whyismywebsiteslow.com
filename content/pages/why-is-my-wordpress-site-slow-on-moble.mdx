## Why WordPress Sites Often Struggle on Mobile
It's rarely one big thing. Usually it's a combination of:
- Heavy, unoptimized images that choke mobile data connections
- Too many plugins running in the background
- Bloated themes (Elementor, Divi, etc.) with lots of animations and scripts
- Missing proper caching or a good CDN for slower networks

The good news? Most of these are fixable pretty quickly once you know where to look.
